Background The Nurturing Early Skills through Technology (NEST) Project is funded by the Japan Social Development Fund (JSDF) and administered by the World Bank. The Project is implemented by Shanti Volunteer Association (SVA) in close collaboration with the Ministry of Education, Youth and Sport (MoEYS) of Cambodia. The Project aims to enhance access to and quality of Early Childhood Education (ECE) through the promotion of learning through play and the integration of digital technology in target districts in Cambodia. The Project supports teacher professional development, establishment of an ECE digital resource platform, parental and community engagement, improvement of preschool learning environments through construction and renovation, and implementation of pilot school meal programs. The Project is implemented in selected districts in Battambang, Banteay Meanchey, and Pailin provinces, targeting public preschools with the objective of strengthening equitable access to quality ECE services for young children aged 3–5 years. Under Sub-Component 1.2, the Project will establish an ECE Digital Resource Platform integrated with MoEYS digital systems and platforms. The platform will serve as a centralized repository of ECE teaching and learning resources, including lesson planner, play-based learning materials, classroom activity ideas, videos, guidebooks, and other digital contents aligned with the national ECE curriculum. These resources are intended to support preschool teachers in applying child-centered and play-based teaching approaches in their daily classroom practices. In addition, the Project will support the development of e-learning modules for preschool teachers and principals through the MoEYS Capacity Development Platform. The modules will contribute to continuous professional development (CPD) by providing structured online learning opportunities related to ECE pedagogy, play-based learning, classroom management, child development, and integration of digital resources into teaching practices. The Project also promotes the appropriate use of digital technology to support teachers’ professional learning, mentoring, and access to educational resources, particularly in rural and underserved areas. To support the effective implementation of these activities, SVA seeks to recruit a qualified National Individual Consultant – ECE Digital Platform and Content Development Specialist. 2. Objectives The objective of this consultancy is to support the development and implementation of digital systems and digital learning contents under the NEST Project to strengthen the quality of Early Childhood Education (ECE) teacher professional development in Cambodia. The ECE Digital Platform and Content Development Specialist will provide technical and coordination support for the establishment of the ECE Digital Resource Platform integrated with existing MoEYS systems, as well as the development of E-learning modules and digital learning resources for preschool teachers and principals. The consultancy aims to improve teachers’ access to practical, curriculum-aligned, and play-based teaching resources and to strengthen continuous professional development (CPD) opportunities through appropriate and sustainable use of digital technology. The assignment will also support the effective integration of digital tools and contents into teacher training, mentoring, and classroom practices, particularly in rural and underserved preschool settings. 3. Scope of Work Under the supervision of the NEST Project Manager and Senior Coordinator and in close coordination with Task Team, and other relevant stakeholders, the ECE Digital Platform and Content Development Specialist will undertake the following tasks: a. Digital Platform Development and Coordination Ensure integration and compatibility with existing MoEYS digital systems and platforms. Support preparation of functional and technical requirements for the ECE Digital Resource Platform. Coordinate with the contracted IT firm and relevant stakeholders to ensure that platform features meet operational requirements. Provide technical input to improve platform usability, accessibility, and sustainability. b. Development of ECE Digital Learning Contents Lead the development, organization, and digitalization of ECE teaching and learning resources, including classroom activity resources, videos, guidebooks, lesson planners. Lead and support the design of structured play-based learning modules aligned with national ECE priorities and curriculum frameworks. Ensure alignment with Continuous Professional Development (CPD) frameworks and PSTTC standards. c. Coordination and Consultation Facilitate technical working sessions and coordination meetings with Task Team members including MoEYS departments and relevant stakeholders. Support organization and facilitation of national consultation workshops, review meetings, and validation processes related to digital platforms and e-learning contents. d. Capacity Building and Dissemination Develop user manuals and simple guidance materials for teachers, trainers, and stakeholders. Support teacher training sessions related to the digital platform and E-learning modules. Provide ongoing technical support and troubleshooting during implementation. e. Reporting Prepare an inception report, periodic progress reports, summarizing activities, achievements, challenges, and recommendations. Document lessons learned and provide technical recommendations for sustainability and future scale-up. 4.
