DIGITAL ZAMBIA ACCELERATION PROJECT (DZAP) REQUEST FOR EXPRESSIONS OF INTEREST (CONSULTING SERVICES – FIRMS SELECTION) COUNTRY: Zambia NAME OF PROJECT: The Digital Zambia Acceleration Project (DZAP) Loan No./Credit No./ Grant No.: P505094 Name of Implementing Agency: Smart Zambia Institute (SZI) Assignment Title: Consulting Services for the Design, Piloting, Implementation and Scale-Up of the Digital Ambassadors Program (DAP) Reference No.: ZM-SZ-554960-CS-QCBS Background The Government of the Republic of Zambia (GRZ) has received financing from the World Bank to accelerate countrywide digital transformation through the Digital Zambia Acceleration Project (DZAP) and intends to use part of the proceeds of the credit to accelerate country-wide digital transformation under the Digital Zambia Acceleration Project (DZAP) and intends to apply part of the proceeds for Consulting Services for the Design, Piloting, Implementation and Scale-Up of the Digital Ambassadors Program (DAP). Objectives of the assignment The overall objective of this consultancy is to design, pilot, and prepare for scale-up of a national and inclusive approach to digital literacy and online-safety competencies among underserved Zambians, across a range of core services. This will include exploration of the role and design of a Digital Ambassadors Program and Digital Transformation Center network, as well as other potential alternatives for scaling of digital literacy, based on exploration of the needs, conditions and resources of different groups of Zambians. This will include exploration of options for financial sustainability over the long term. Capacity building and transfer of knowledge are integral, cross-cutting objectives of this assignment. The Scope of services The Scope of services shall include undertaking the full set of tasks described below. The scope is organized in five (5) work streams. i. Work Stream A - DAP Model Refinement and Institutional Framework; ii. Work Stream B - Digital Literacy Curriculum and Training Package; iii. Work Stream C - DAP Pilot Design, Rollout and Evaluation; iv. Work Stream D - DTC Scale-Up Assessment and Implementation Plan; and v. Work Stream E - Financial Sustainability Model and Bidding Documents. Invitation to eligible Consulting Firms The SZI now invites eligible consulting firms (“Consultants”) to indicate their interest in providing the Services by submitting Expressions of Interest (EOI). Interested Consultants should submit EOI complete with information demonstrating that they have the required qualifications and relevant experience to perform the required Services.
