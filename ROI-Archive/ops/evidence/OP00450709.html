Kingdom of Cambodia Nation Religion King Ministry of Economy and Finance National Payment Certification Agency (NPCA) Health Equity and Quality Improvement Project Phase 2 (H EQIP 2) REQUEST FOR EXPRESSIONS OF INTEREST (INDIVIDUAL CONSULTANT) Two (2) Senior Analysts (Data Claim Analysis for HEF) Ref: H EQIP II ICS 26/003 Background The Health Equity and Quality Improvement Project Phase 2 (HEQIP 2) support the Royal Government of Cambodia in advancing Universal Health Coverage (UHC), improving financial protection and access to services for the poor and vulnerable, and strengthening the health system. The project is funded by the World Bank, Pooling Partners (DFAT, GFF, KfW, KOICA), and the Royal Government of Cambodia. Following the MidTerm Review, it was identified that while annual Health Equity Fund (HEF) expenditure exceeds US$20 million, the utilization of HEF claim data remains limited. The National Payment Certification Agency (NPCA) manages the Patient Management and Registration System (PMRS), which records HEF claim data nationwide. To address this, NPCA seeks to engage two (2) Senior Analysts (Data Claim Analysis for HEF) to enhance monitoring and reporting of HEF utilization through advanced data analysis and to build internal analytical capacity. Contract Duration The Senior Analysts will be contracted for a period of twelve (12) months from the contract signing date, with the possibility of extension depending on satisfactory performance, project needs, and fund availability. The contract is subject to quarterly performance reviews. The duty station will be at NPCA headquarters in Phnom Penh, with frequent travel to provinces as required. Minimum Qualifications and Experience Required Education: At least a Bachelor’s Degree in Epidemiology, Public Health, Health Economics, or a related field. Professional Experience: At least 3 years of professional experience in epidemiology, health data analysis, and reporting, preferably within health systems or social protection programs. At least 3 years of experience in health data management, claims analysis, and program monitoring. Technical Skills: Good proficiency in statistical and analytical software such as R, Stata, SQL, or Python . At least 2 projects demonstrating proven experience with Health Equity Fund (HEF) operations, health financing, or social protection schemes. Language Proficiency: Good analytical, report writing, presentation, and communication skills in both English and Khmer , with the ability to convey complex technical concepts to both technical and nontechnical audiences. Collaboration: Ability to work closely with an International Consultant and with NPCA staff to transfer knowledge and institutionalize data management practices. Scope of Work (Summary) The Senior Analysts will be responsible for: Collecting, cleaning, validating, and organizing HEF claims data from PMRS and other sources. Analyzing trends in service utilization, claim submission, approval, and reimbursement. Producing comprehensive analytical reports and providing actionable, evidencebased recommendations. Advising NPCA staff on epidemiological methods, data analysis, and visualization techniques. Conducting training or mentorship sessions on data management and analysis. Collaborating daily with the International Data Claim Review Consultant, providing local context and data validation support. Designing and institutionalizing data cleaning and reporting workflows within the Department of Policy.
