REPUBLIC OF MALAWI MINISTRY OF ENERGY AND MINING DEPARTMENT OF ENERGY AFFAIRS REQUEST FOR EXPRESSION OF INTEREST (CONSULTING SERVICES –FIRM SELECTION) Country Malawi Project Name ASCENT Malawi Project Project Number P502464 Assignment Title Consultancy Services for Consumer Awareness Campaign for Ascent Malawi Project Reference No. MW-MOE. -552895-CS-QCBS EOI Issue Date 5th June 2026 This request for expression of interest follows the General Procurement Notice for this Project that appeared in the United Nations Development Business (UNDB) on 31st January 2025. The Government of the Republic of Malawi through the Ministry of Energy and Mining (MoEM) has received financing from the International Development Association (IDA) and Energy Sector Management Assistance Program (ESMAP) towards the cost of implementing the Accelerating Sustainable and Clean Energy Access Transformation in Malawi (ASCENT Malawi) Project. It is intended that part of the proceeds of the financing will be used to cover eligible payments under the contracts for Consultancy services. The consulting services (“the Services”) intent is to engage a qualified firm to provide Consumer Awareness Consultancy Services for the following components of the ASCENT Malawi Project: Component 1 – Last Mile Grid Connections; Component 2 – Off-Grid Connections through Solar Home Systems; Component 3 – Energy Access for Schools and Health Facilities; and Component 4 – Clean Cooking Solutions. The objective of the assignment is to engage a firm to design and implement a comprehensive, evidence-based consumer awareness, behavior change, and project publicity campaign that ensures nationwide visibility of the ASCENT Malawi Project and all its components. The campaign shall promote informed adoption, safe use, and sustainable management of off-grid solar systems, grid electricity, solar systems in public institutions, and clean cooking solutions, while addressing cross-cutting issues of theft and vandalism, gender equality and social inclusion, and environmental and social safeguards. The scope of work under this assignment shall include the development, launch, and implementation of an integrated Consumer Awareness, Behavior Change, and Project Publicity Campaign, including but not limited to the following: Consumer Awareness and Communications Strategy Media and Publicity Strategy Stakeholder Engagement Strategy Integrated communication strategy Behaviour Change Framework or Theory of Change The detailed Terms of Reference (TORs) for the assignment can be found at the following websites: Ministry of Energy and Mining (https://energy.gov.mw/resources/), Ngwee Ngwee Ngwee Fund (https://www.nnnf.energy.gov.mw/resources/) and ASCENT Malawi Project (https://www.ascent.mw/resources/) or upon request through the following email address amopiha@ascent.mw/amopiha@gmail.com copy to info@ascent.mw. All requests for clarification should be sent through above emails, and the Client will respond to the requested clarifications.
