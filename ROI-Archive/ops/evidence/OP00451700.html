Section I. Invitation to Bid Invitation to Bid for CONCRETING OF BANGILAN-BANNAWAG-PISAN FARM TO MARKET ROAD WITH BRIDGE Subproject ID No. PRDP-SU-IB-R12-COT-006-KAB-001-2024-FMB 1) The Government of the Philippines (GOP) has received a Loan from the World Bank toward the cost of Philippine Rural Development Project Scale Up, and it intends to apply part of the proceeds of this Loan to payments under the contract for Concreting of Bangilan-Bannawag-Pisan Farm to Market Road with Bridge with Subproject ID No. PRDP-SU-IB-R12-COT-006-KAB-001-2024- FMB. 2) The Local Government Unit of Kabacan, implementing partner of the Department of Agriculture, now invites bids for the Concreting of Bangilan- Bannawag-Pisan Farm to Market Road with Bridge with Estimated Project Cost (EPC) in the amount of One Hundred Seventy-Nine Million Two Hundred Seventy-Three Thousand Pesos Only (Php179,273,000.00). Completion of the Works is required in 396 calendar days. Bidders should have completed, within the last ten (10) years, a contract similar to the Project. The description of an eligible bidder is contained in the Bidding Documents, particularly, in Section 5 of the Instructions to Bidders, and Sub-sections 5.2 and 5.4 of the Bid Data Sheet. 3) Bidding will be conducted in accordance with relevant procedures for open competitive bidding as specified in the Implementing Rules and Regulations (IRR) of Republic Act (RA) 12009, otherwise known as the “New Government Procurement Reform Act”, with some amendments, as stated in the Bidding Documents and is open to all Bidders from eligible source countries, as defined in the World Bank Procurement Regulations. The contract shall be awarded to the Bidder that met the criteria indicated in the Bidding Documents. 4. Interested bidders may obtain further information from the Local Government Unit of Kabacan and inspect the Bidding Documents at the address given below from 8:00 a.m. to 5:00 p.m. 5. A complete set of Bidding Documents may be acquired by interested bidders starting on June 17, 2026, from the address below and upon payment or depositing to the LGU KAB-Trust Fund, Land Bank of the Philippines (LBP, Kabacan Branch), Account Number: 2732-1000-60 of the Bidding Documents fees of Ten Thousand Pesos (PhP10,000.00). The LGU Treasurer’s official receipt or the bank teller’s validated deposit slip or printed receipt from online payment channels serves as the proof of payment. It may also be downloaded free of charge from the website of the Philippine Government Electronic Procurement System (PhilGEPS) and the PRDP website (prdp.da.gov.ph), provided that bidders shall pay the said Bidding Documents fee not later than the submission of their bids.
